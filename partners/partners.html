Any Questions?

Why Should I partner with Foodpanda?
  • Foodpanda helps businesses grow their sales,
    reach new customers, and increase brand awareness
    By partnering with us, you can get access to new customers who
    want to order from businesses like yours
    for delivery and pickup.
How do I become a foodpanda Partner?
  • Register your interest on the form above
    and ensure you have a valid phone number
    and email address. We will guide you
    throughout your registration
    journey via email and/or over the phone.
Why do you charge commission fee?
  • The commission fee collected ensures our delivery partners are
    fairly paid and enables us to provide platform services
    for online ordering and delivery services
Will my outlet be automatically eligible for pick-up?
  • All of our partners are able to accept pick-up orders from their
    first day to provide you access to more order opportunities.
What is the delivery radius of foodpanda delivery?
  • Our delivery radius is up to 5km from the delivery address.
    This is to ensure more restaurants and shops are closer
    to their customer while keeping consumer orders
    fresh to consumer doorstep.
Can I stop orders if it gets too busy?
  • Yes, our partners can pause orders at any time. Using
    our tablet, you can pause your store from collecting orders
    for a specific time period. You can also indicate that
    your store is busy, letting customers know
    to expect longer prep times.
